MY PICK → 02 / DRIFT wins as a Twitter PFP. It's the simplest of the three, which means it survives the 48px circle crop — you still read "a" + amber dot. The rotation and the escaped dot give it personality without relying on texture that gets lost at small sizes. 01 / MISPRINT is the most "artist" but the chromatic offset muddies at 48px. 03 / CONTAMINATION is gorgeous at full size but visually collapses in a circle crop — better as a banner or landing-page hero than a PFP.
