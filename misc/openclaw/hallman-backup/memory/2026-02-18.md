## Genre Deep-Dive #16: UK Garage / 2-Step
- Added `uk_garage` entry to `genres-deep.json` (now 16 genres total)
- Covers speed garage AND 2-step variants
- Key focus: the skippy kick pattern (kick on 1 but NOT on 3), displaced to offbeat positions
- Drum pattern: kick on steps 0, 6, 10, 12 — step 8 (beat 3) deliberately empty
- Swing: 60% on 16th-note hi-hats — the narrow sweet spot for UKG groove
- Bass: sine sub (2-step) vs detuned saw Reese (speed garage)
- Vocal chops pitched +2 to +5 semitones — Todd Edwards micro-sampling technique
- Garage organ stabs on offbeats
- Reference tracks: MJ Cole "Sincere", Todd Edwards, Double 99 "Ripgroove", Artful Dodger, El-B, Wookie, Zed Bias
- Lineage: American garage house → speed garage → 2-step → dubstep/grime
